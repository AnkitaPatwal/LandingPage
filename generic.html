Infrasound Detection System: Collaborated with NASA Space Grant Consortium

(May 2024 - Present)

Collaborated on the development of an advanced infrasound detection system through a research initiative with NASA’s Space Grant Consortium. The project focused on utilizing Raspberry Pi technology to enhance the detection and analysis of low-frequency sounds. It also involved employing the bass cannon method to generate specific infrasound frequencies, contributing to innovative approaches in seismic activity detection.

Enhanced precision in detecting infrasound and contributed to earthquake detection. The project aims to improve these methods further, with a future focus on detecting seismic activity on Venus.

Vice President, Society of Women Engineers (SWE)

California State University - Sacramento (March 2024 – Present)

As Vice President of the Society of Women Engineers (SWE), I spearheaded initiatives to promote STEM education and support women in engineering. I was responsible for organizing events, managing outreach efforts, and fostering community engagement. Notable activities included a Panel Discussion on Women in the Workplace, where I organized and moderated a session with successful women across industries, focusing on challenges, achievements, and strategies for advancing women professionally. Additionally, I coordinated Women's Shadow Day, an event for elementary and high school students to explore engineering through campus tours, interactive sessions, and panels with students and professionals.
